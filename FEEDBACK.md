## 🏆 Most Impactful Feedback & What Changed

These are the highest-signal responses from beta testers — the ones that directly shaped improvements made to Fintrix.

---

### 🐛 1. Marketplace Performance Lag

<table>
<tr>
<th>🗣️ Feedback Received</th>
<th>✅ What Was Done</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>

**Shubhangi Agale** ⭐ 4/5 · Bug Report

> *"The marketplace section is lagging."*

</td>
<td>

**Marketplace page performance optimised.**
Lazy-loading and pagination added to the invoice listing. Heavy deal cards are now rendered incrementally rather than all at once, reducing initial load time and eliminating the lag on the marketplace route.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

---

### 🧭 2. Onboarding Clarity for First-Time Users

<table>
<tr>
<th>🗣️ Feedback Received</th>
<th>✅ What Was Done</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>

**Sumit Borhade** ⭐ 5/5 · Comment

> *"Some parts were slightly confusing, especially for first-time users, due to limited explanations and guidance during certain steps."*

Suggestions included: step-by-step walkthrough at the start, tooltips for difficult concepts, clearer instructions, and real-life case studies.

</td>
<td>

**In-app onboarding flow added.**
A guided walkthrough was introduced at the start of the simulation. Tooltip overlays were added to key actions (e.g. Trust Score dimensions, yield calculation). The Resources section was expanded with real-world invoice financing examples and a terminology glossary.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

---

### 🤖 3. AI-Assisted Invoice Auto-Fill

<table>
<tr>
<th>🗣️ Feedback Received</th>
<th>✅ What Was Done</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>

**Anushka Kachare** ⭐ 4/5 · Feature Request

> *"I feel like the AI chatbot should also fill in the invoices."*

</td>
<td>

**OCR auto-population enhanced.**
The existing OCR pipeline already extracts vendor name, amount, dates, and reference numbers from uploaded invoices. Based on this feedback, the extracted fields are now automatically pre-filled into the invoice form, reducing manual entry to zero for well-formatted documents. The AI assistant was also updated to guide users through incomplete fields.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

---

### 📄 4. Invoice Input Format — PDFs vs Alternatives

<table>
<tr>
<th>🗣️ Feedback Received</th>
<th>✅ What Was Done</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>

**Shekhar Agale** ⭐ 4/5 · Question

> *"Are PDFs really the best way to put invoices?"*

</td>
<td>

**Multi-format support confirmed and documented.**
Fintrix already supports PDF, PNG, and JPG formats. Clearer messaging was added to the upload UI to surface this — users now see explicit format options and a file-size guide (max 10MB) before uploading. A future iteration may explore direct invoice form entry as an alternative to file upload.

</td>
</tr>
</table>
